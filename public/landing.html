WHAT WE BELIEVE · §02

The web should have public spaces again.

01 / FREE FOREVER

No plans. No paywalls.

Every tool, every feature, every room. Always. The product doesn't change based on who you are or who's paying.

02 / NO ACCOUNTS

Identity is optional.

You don't sign up. You don't get tracked. You show up, you make something, you leave. The way the internet used to feel.

03 / EPHEMERAL BY DESIGN

Rooms forget. People remember.

Boards reset every 24 hours of inactivity. Save what matters as a snapshot. Let the rest go - that's the point.

04 / PUBLIC BY DEFAULT

Anyone. Anywhere.

If you know the URL, you're in the room. No invites, no permission walls, no waiting rooms. A park bench, not a private club.
