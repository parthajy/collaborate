Changelog

Every change to collaborate.so, newest first. The product is free, public, and has no accounts - so this page is the record.

v1.0 2026-05-22

Public launch

  • Open any room at collaborate.so/your-word - no login required.
  • Real-time multiplayer: live cursors, presence, and edits that sync instantly.
  • Pen, marker, eraser, text, sticky notes, shapes, flow nodes, images, video and link cards.
  • Infinite pan and zoom canvas with PNG snapshot export.
  • Rooms self-destroy 24 hours after they go quiet.
v0.9 2026-05-15

Open beta

  • Character-level merge for text and sticky notes - two people can type in the same note.
  • Live ink streaming - watch other people draw stroke by stroke.
  • Per-room abuse limits and a room-name filter.
v0.5 2026-05-01

Private alpha

  • First working canvas with CRDT-based sync.
  • Mobile and desktop support.
